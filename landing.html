The gateway

Protection happens before execution, not after damage.

Compass is not a dashboard that warns later. It is the gate between agent intent and money movement.

Mandate

Define what the agent may do.

Limits, destinations, tools, tokens, timing and approval requirements become the operating charter.

  • spend caps
  • allowlists
  • slippage rules
Evaluation

Inspect the proposed action.

Compass decodes the request, checks policy and classifies risk before the signer is involved.

  • transfer
  • swap
  • tool call
Record

Leave a traceable decision.

Approvals, blocks and escalations are written with context so teams can review what happened.

  • decision reason
  • policy match
  • audit log
